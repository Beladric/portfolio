Information Communication and Technologies

ICT for Information Communication Technologies or its old name DDCE for Digital Data & Connectivity Engineering, is the entity which aim to answer internal needs. Each year, departments which compose Stellantis received a budget. When one entity have digital needs they contact ICT, which will invoice entity buyer. As long as it is possible ICT choose to outsource the development of applications because Stellantis can't pretend to have same skills internally as companies specialized in computer science. Nevertheless, in some cases development is made in internal, in particular for very critical applications or very specialized applications which need huge Stellantis background. Moreover, all ICT members have huge skills in computer science, because they coordinate subcontractors and take care to not be fooled.
